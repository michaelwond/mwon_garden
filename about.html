About

I'm an engineer and builder specializing in deployment systems. I spend my time helping teams adopt tools that change how software gets made.

Through all of it, I've been writing to make sense of what I'm seeing. This site acts as an open-source terminal for my thoughts.

What I Believe

Clarity over complexity. The best ideas are often the simplest ones. The work is finding them.

Building beats planning. You learn more in a week of building than a month of strategizing. Ship early, iterate often.

Writing is thinking. If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough.
